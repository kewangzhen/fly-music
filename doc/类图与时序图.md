### 5.2 时序图 - AI 音乐生成（异步任务）

```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant Client
    participant AiMusicController
    participant AiMusicService
    participant AiMusicGenerationRepository
    participant ThreadPool
    participant MusicGenService

    Note over Client,MusicGenService: 异步任务流程

    rect rgb(230, 245, 255)
        Note over Client,AiMusicGenerationRepository: 阶段1：提交生成任务
        Client->>AiMusicController: POST /api/ai/generate
        Note right of Client: prompt=欢快的歌曲<br/>genre=流行<br/>mood=欢快<br/>duration=60
        AiMusicController->>AiMusicService: generateMusicAsync(userId, prompt, genre, mood, duration)
        AiMusicService->>AiMusicGenerationRepository: save(generation with status=0)
        Note right of AiMusicGenerationRepository: status=0 表示生成中
        AiMusicGenerationRepository-->>AiMusicService: saved generation
        AiMusicService-->>AiMusicController: Future~Generation~
        AiMusicController-->>Client: 202 Accepted
        Note right of Client: 返回任务信息，status=processing
    end

    rect rgb(255, 245, 230)
        Note over ThreadPool,MusicGenService: 阶段2：后台异步执行
        ThreadPool-->>AiMusicService: 执行异步任务
        AiMusicService->>AiMusicService: callMusicGenService(generation)
        AiMusicService->>MusicGenService: POST /api/generate
        Note right of MusicGenService: prompt=欢快的歌曲<br/>duration=60
        MusicGenService-->>AiMusicService: task_id=xxx (异步任务ID)
        Note right of AiMusicService: 任务ID返回，立即返回
    end

    rect rgb(245, 255, 230)
        Note over AiMusicService,MusicGenService: 阶段3：轮询等待生成完成
        loop 轮询任务状态 (最多120次，每次5秒)
            AiMusicService->>MusicGenService: GET /api/status/{task_id}
            MusicGenService-->>AiMusicService: status=processing
        end
        AiMusicService->>MusicGenService: GET /api/status/{task_id}
        MusicGenService-->>AiMusicService: status=completed, audio_url=xxx
        AiMusicService->>AiMusicService: downloadAndSaveAudio(audio_url, generation_id)
        AiMusicService->>AiMusicGenerationRepository: save(generation with status=1, musicUrl)
        Note right of AiMusicGenerationRepository: status=1 表示已完成
    end

    rect rgb(255, 230, 245)
        Note over Client,AiMusicGenerationRepository: 阶段4：查询任务状态
        Client->>AiMusicController: GET /api/ai/generate/{id}
        AiMusicController->>AiMusicService: getGenerationStatus(id)
        AiMusicService->>AiMusicGenerationRepository: findById(id)
        AiMusicGenerationRepository-->>AiMusicService: generation
        alt 任务完成
            AiMusicService-->>AiMusicController: generation with status=1, musicUrl
            AiMusicController-->>Client: 200 OK (已完成)
        else 任务进行中
            AiMusicService-->>AiMusicController: generation with status=0
            AiMusicController-->>Client: 200 OK (处理中)
        else 任务失败
            AiMusicService-->>AiMusicController: generation with status=2, errorMessage

        end
    end
```
